SS Wire Mesh – Durable & Precision Engineered

Our Stainless Steel (SS) Wire Mesh is crafted for strength, longevity, and superior performance across industrial, commercial, and domestic applications. Designed with high-quality stainless steel, this wire mesh resists corrosion, heat, and wear, making it perfect for filtration, sieving, screening, fencing, and protective applications.
Whether you need with clamp for secure installations or without clamp for flexible use, our SS wire mesh adapts to your specific requirements. It ensures reliable functionality while maintaining a clean, professional appearance.

Key Features:

  • ✅Material: Premium Stainless Steel (SS 304 / SS 316 options available)
  • ✅High Durability: Resistant to corrosion, rust, and extreme temperatures
  • ✅Customizable Sizes: Available in multiple widths, lengths, and mesh openings
  • ✅Clamp Options: With clamp for secure fitting, without clamp for flexibility
  • ✅Versatile Applications: Ideal for filtration, sieving, fencing, screening, and industrial uses
  • ✅Low Maintenance: Easy to clean and long-lasting

Available Sizes:

  • Standard widths: 1 ft, 2 ft, 3 ft, or custom sizes
  • Mesh openings: Fine to coarse (customizable as per requirement)

Why Choose Our SS Wire Mesh:

  • High precision in mesh weaving ensures consistent performance
  • Corrosion-resistant and maintenance-free
  • Robust, long-lasting, and ideal for industrial or domestic applications
