Casting & Mechanical Items – Precision & Durability for Industrial Needs

We supply a wide range of Casting and Mechanical Items designed to meet the demanding needs of industries such as cement, steel, mining, power plants, and engineering works. Manufactured from high-grade alloys, stainless steel, and cast iron, these components are engineered for strength, accuracy, and long service life.
Our casting products ensure dimensional accuracy, while mechanical items are designed for heavy-duty performance. With customization options available, we deliver components that perfectly fit your industrial machinery and systems.

  • ◆ CI / SG Iron Castings – Strong, durable, and ideal for heavy-duty machinery.
  • ◆ Steel Castings – Precision-engineered for high wear resistance and performance.

  • ◆ Mechanical Spares & Components – Gears, pulleys, couplings, shafts, and other precision parts.
