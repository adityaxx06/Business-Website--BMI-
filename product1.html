Spring Steel Wire Mesh – Built for Strength and Reliability

Our Spring Steel Wire Mesh is designed for heavy-duty screening applications in sponge iron plants, cement industries, power plants, and mining operations. Manufactured using Grade-1 Spring Steel (Usha Martin/Tata), it offers exceptional strength, flexibility, and long-lasting performance in demanding environments.
We supply the mesh in all standard and customized sizes, ensuring a perfect fit for your plant requirements. To match diverse applications, it is available with clamps and without clamps, giving you flexibility in installation and usage.

Key Features

  • ✅ High Strength & Durability – Made from premium Grade-1 Spring Steel.
  • ✅ Wide Size Range – Available in all standard and customized dimensions.
  • ✅ Clamp Options – Supplied with clamps for easy installation or without clamps as per customer need.
  • ✅ Industrial Applications – Ideal for sponge iron, cement, mining, and power plants.
  • ✅ Reliable Performance – Ensures long service life, reduced downtime, and consistent screening.
