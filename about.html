About Balaji Metal Industries

Decades of reliability supplying critical components to sponge iron, cement and power plants across India — from spring steel screens to SS wire mesh, conveyor idlers, kiln refractory anchors and precision castings.

Who We Are

Balaji Metal Industries (BMI) is a trusted manufacturer and supplier of high-performance industrial components. We combine quality raw materials (Usha Martin/Tata Grade-1 wires), modern fabrication and strict QA to deliver reliable parts that keep your plants running with minimal downtime.
