SS Anchor Klin – Strong & Reliable Refractory Fixing Solution

Our Stainless Steel (SS) Anchor Klin is designed to provide strong support and reliable fixing for refractory linings in kilns, furnaces, and high-temperature industrial equipment. Manufactured from high-grade stainless steel, these anchors ensure stability, durability, and resistance against heat, corrosion, and mechanical stress.
Available in different shapes, sizes, and grades (SS 304, SS 310, SS 316, and other alloys), the SS Anchor Klin is ideal for securing castable refractories, ceramic fiber linings, and insulation boards. Its precise design ensures long service life and reduced maintenance in thermal and chemical processing industries.

Key Features:

  • ✅Material: High-quality Stainless Steel (SS 304 / SS 310 / SS 316)
  • ✅Heat Resistance: Withstands extreme temperatures in kilns and furnaces
  • ✅Corrosion Resistant: Durable in chemical and thermal environments
  • ✅Multiple Shapes: V, Y, U, and custom designs available
  • ✅Strong Support: Provides stability to refractory linings
  • ✅Custom Sizes: Designed as per project specifications

Available Sizes & Options:

  • Standard thickness: 3mm, 4mm, 5mm, or custom
  • Lengths: 50mm to 300mm (customizable)
  • Shapes: V, Y, U, Zig-Zag, or as per requirement

Why Choose Our SS Anchor Klin:

  • Long-lasting and maintenance-free
  • Precision engineered for strong refractory fixing
  • Suitable for high-temperature & corrosive environments
  • Wide range of sizes and shapes available
