Conveyor Idlers & Rollers – Smooth & Efficient Material Handling

Our Conveyor Idlers & Rollers are engineered for seamless material flow in mining, bulk handling, and industrial conveyor systems. Designed with precision and high-quality materials, these components reduce friction, enhance belt life, and ensure smooth operation even under heavy loads.
Available in various diameters, lengths, and bearing types, our conveyor idlers and rollers can be customized for impact, carrying, and return applications. They are ideal for industries like mining, cement, power plants, ports, and manufacturing units.

Key Features:

  • ✅Material: High-grade steel with corrosion-resistant coatings
  • ✅Durability: Designed to withstand heavy loads and harsh conditions
  • ✅Smooth Operation: Reduces friction and minimizes belt wear
  • ✅Custom Sizes: Various diameters and lengths to suit different conveyor systems
  • ✅Bearing Options: Sealed or open bearings for different environments
  • ✅Versatile Applications: Suitable for mining, bulk material handling, and industrial conveyors

Available Sizes & Options:

  • Standard diameters: 76mm, 89mm, 108mm, 133mm, or custom
  • Lengths: From 300mm up to 2000mm (or as per requirement)
  • Bearing types: Sealed, open, or heavy-duty bearings

Why Choose Our Conveyor Idlers & Rollers:

  • Reduces downtime by enhancing conveyor efficiency
  • Corrosion-resistant and low maintenance
  • Precision-engineered for long-lasting performance
